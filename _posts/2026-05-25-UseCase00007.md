**User Story:** As an academic podcast producer I want to provide additional context so that my listeners can gain further insight into my subject matter.

**Description:** Citations, definitions, and additional resources can be added as annotations to an academic podcast, providing listeners with further reading and additional context. Relevant to any fields producing academic podcasts and audio content.

**Ingredients**
- 1 academic podcast
- Citations, definitions, additional resources, etc.
- Tags (optional)

**Method**
1. Gather all citations and other resources, with associated **timestamps** for each piece of information
2. Upload academic podcast to AVAnnotate and add citations and other resources as **annotations**, following [Quick Start: How to use AVAnnotate](https://av-annotate.org/how-to-use/) guide
3. Create and add **tags** to annotations, if desired, following Quick Start guide

**Example:** [Sound and Seconds: A Roundtable on Timestamping for Literary Archives](https://ndamours14.github.io/sound-and-seconds/) by Natasha D’Amours
