**User Story:** As an audiovisual artist, I want to create an annotated portfolio of my work so that I can share my works with potential clients, employers, and collaborators.

**Description:** Artists, designers, academics, or anyone who creates audiovisual works can benefit from creating a portfolio to showcase their works. AVAnnotate allows these works to be annotated, providing additional context and information about the works. Relevant fields include Visual Arts, Music, and Film Production, as well as any field that produces academic podcasts or videos.

**Ingredients**
- A collection of personal audiovisual works
- Annotations for each work
- Biographical information, information about your work, and contact information
- Tags (optional)

**Method**
1. Gather your works - your portfolio may be a comprehensive collection of all of your works, or a curated collection of selected works
2. Generate information about each of your audiovisual works - this can be production information, additional context, or the annotations may be part of the work itself
3. Use this information to create annotations, with associated timestamps for each piece of information
4. Upload audiovisual works to AVAnnotate and add information as annotations, following Quick Start: How to use AVAnnotate guide
5. Create and add tags to annotations, if desired, following Quick Start guide
6. Create a home page with biographical information, information about your work, and contact information
