**User Story:** As a poet and a scholar, I want to create transcriptions of poetry recordings so that I can increase the accessibility and searchability of these recordings.

**Description:** Transcripts of audio and/or video recordings can be added to the recordings as annotations, improving both the accessibility and the searchability of the recordings. Relevant to any fields producing audio and/or video content.

**Ingredients**
- 1 or more audio and/or video recordings
- Transcripts of recording(s)
- Tags (optional)

**Method**
1. Generate transcripts of recording(s) if they do not already exist, making note of associated **timestamps**
- Tools like AssemblyAI can be used to generate a transcript of a recording - if using an AI-generated transcript, it should be verified and edited by a human for accuracy
2. Upload audio recording(s) to AVAnnotate and add transcripts as **annotations**, following [Quick Start: How to use AVAnnotate](https://av-annotate.org/how-to-use/) guide
3. Create and add **tags** to annotations, if desired, following Quick Start guide

**Example:** [Furious Flower Poetry Center Transcriptions](https://jmu-audiannotate.github.io/FFPC-Video-Transcriptions/) by Evan Sizemore
