**User Story:** As a poet, I want to create a video anthology of my poetry so that I can share my poems in a multi-sensory, multimedia format.

**Description:** Poetry anthologies can exist beyond the written word. A poetry anthology can be made up of a collection of recorded performances, with annotations that include the written poems as well as reflections on the poems and the performances. This anthology can be made up of poems written by the performer, or existing poems being collected and read by the performer. Relevant fields include English, Literature, Poetry, and Writing.

**Ingredients**
- Collection of poems (these can be poems you have written or a collection of existing poems)
- Recordings of each poem being performed
- Annotations (including the text of each poem as well as reflections upon the poetry and performances)
- Tags (optional)

**Method**
1. Record yourself reading and performing each poem in the collection
2. Make note of reflections on the poems and the performances, with **timestamps** for all notes
  - [This lesson plan](https://jentery.github.io/SWDemo/output_documents/307_Winter_2018_Elocution_Assignment.html) includes further information that may be helpful in describing your reflections
3. Upload recording(s) to AVAnnotate and add **annotations**, following [Quick Start: How to use AVAnnotate](https://av-annotate.org/how-to-use/) guide
  - Annotations can include both a transcript of the poetry being read, as well as reflections
4. Create and add **tags** to annotations, if desired, following Quick Start guide
5. Create a home page, if desired, to introduce the poetry anthology; this may also include the written texts of all of the poems in the collection
