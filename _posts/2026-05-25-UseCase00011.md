**User Story:** As a silent film historian, I want to add annotations to a digitized silent film so that I can document the film’s credits, script, damage to the film, and film techniques.

**Description:** Films, particularly older or lesser-known films, can be supplemented with annotations that provide additional context about the film’s credits, script, historical context, digitization, film techniques, etc. Relevant fields include Film History, Film Production, and Documentary Production.

**Ingredients**
- 1 film
- Annotations
- Tags (optional)

**Method**
1. Gather contextual and supplementary information about the films, its credits, its script, etc., with associated **timestamps** for each piece of information
2. Upload film to AVAnnotate and add informations as **annotations**, following [Quick Start: How to use AVAnnotate](https://av-annotate.org/how-to-use/) guide
3. Create and add **tags** to annotations, if desired, following Quick Start guide

**Example:** [Annotating Camille (1921)](https://avannotate.github.io/camille/) by Janet Reinschmidt
